Student Login Help

How to Log In

You can log in using any of these methods:

Method 1:

Username: Your student ID number (e.g., "18")

Password: Same as your student ID number (e.g., "18")

Method 2:

Username: Student_ID (e.g., "Student_18")

Password: Your student ID number (e.g., "18")

What You Can Do After Login

  • View your game progress
  • See your assessment scores
  • Check your learning history

Need More Help?

If you're having trouble logging in, please contact your teacher.
